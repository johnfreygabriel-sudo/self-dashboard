What is BloomBox?

A glass-front flower locker — not a vending machine

Unlike traditional vending machines, a BloomBox unit is a floor-standing cabinet of individually refrigerated locker compartments, each with a glass door so customers can see exactly what they're buying before they pay.

Bouquets are hand-arranged, placed upright in water-filled vases or wrapped in kraft paper, and kept at 4°C. When a customer selects and pays, just that one door unlocks — the rest stay secure.

  • ✓ Glass-front display — see before you buy
  • ✓ Individual locker doors, PIN or card unlocked
  • ✓ Each compartment independently refrigerated
  • ✓ Sleek matte-black powder-coated steel cabinet
  • ✓ Wheeled base — easy to reposition indoors
  • ✓ Contactless card, Apple Pay & Google Pay

Frequently Asked Questions

Each compartment is independently refrigerated and held at 4°C. Large and medium compartments include a built-in water tray so stems can drink. Small compartments use sealed water-tube inserts. Combined with our 48-hour restock cycle, every bouquet is in peak condition.
The customer taps the compartment number on the central touchscreen, reviews the bouquet details and price, and pays by contactless card or phone. The moment payment confirms, an electronic latch releases just that one door — it clicks open a few centimetres. The customer opens it fully, lifts out the flowers, and closes it again.
BloomBox handles everything. Our local floral partners visit every 48 hours to replace sold or ageing stock. Remote diagnostics alert our team to any technical issues before they affect customers. Your venue staff never need to touch the machine.
Any unsold bouquets approaching the 48-hour mark are removed at restock and donated to local care homes, hospitals, and charities. We have a zero-waste policy — flowers always go to someone who can enjoy them.
The cabinet is 180×130×55cm (H×W×D) and runs from a standard 240V/13A plug socket. It requires roughly 1.5m of clear frontage so customers can comfortably browse the glass doors. The wheeled base makes repositioning straightforward.
Venue partners receive 15–20% of gross sales, paid monthly. There are no upfront costs, installation fees, or maintenance charges. BloomBox covers all hardware, stocking, and servicing. Locations with 300+ daily foot traffic typically see strong returns from the first month.
