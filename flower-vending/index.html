What is BloomBox?

A florist in a machine — without the florist hours

BloomBox is a network of refrigerated flower vending machines stocked fresh every 48 hours. Each machine holds up to 200 individual stems and pre-made bouquets, kept at the perfect temperature so every petal arrives in perfect condition.

Whether it's a last-minute anniversary gift, a “thinking of you” bundle, or a single stem to brighten someone's day — BloomBox is there when traditional florists are not.

  • ✓ Refrigerated to 4°C for maximum freshness
  • ✓ Contactless card & mobile pay
  • ✓ Sourced from local & sustainable farms
  • ✓ Real-time inventory on the BloomBox app

Frequently Asked Questions

Every machine is restocked within 48 hours, and our refrigeration units maintain a constant 4°C. Flowers are cut-to-order at our partner farms, rushed to distribution hubs, and loaded into machines within 24 hours of cutting. You can always check the stocking date on the machine's screen.
All BloomBox machines accept credit/debit cards, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and Samsung Pay via NFC tap. We do not accept cash. Some machines also support BloomBox app credits which you can top up online.
Snap a photo and submit a refund request through the BloomBox app or website within 24 hours. We process full refunds within one business day — no questions asked. We take pride in the quality of every bloom.

We partner exclusively with Rainforest Alliance–certified farms and prioritize local growers within 150 miles where possible. Our packaging is 100% compostable, and unsold flowers are donated to local charities and care homes before restocking.
